Work with us

Jamalco Works with the Early Childhood Commission and The Mico Foundation in the training of Early Childhood Education Teachers in Manchester and Clarendon.

The Mico University College has served Jamaica, the wider Caribbean and beyond for more than 180 years.  This tradition of endurance and resilience in delivering quality teacher education made The Mico an iconic institution of profound worth in the western hemisphere during the 21st century. The Mico Foundation has, however, recognized the impact of the changing nature of this new epoch and is committed to do all that is necessary for the University College to keep pace, ensuring its relevance as an institution of choice for teaching, research and learning. The foundation invites your partnership during this period of change.

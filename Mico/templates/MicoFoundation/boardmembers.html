Board of Directors

The Mico Foundation is supervised by a nine-member Board of Directors. Members to the Board of Directors are drawn in equal number from the Trustees, The University College and the Alumni Association (MOSA. There are two (2) ex-officio members, one of whom is the President of The Mico and the other a member of The Lady Mico Trustees resident in Jamaica, or if not, a person named by the Trustees. A Director is elected to serve for three (3) years in the first instance and is eligible to be re-elected to serve for a maximum of two (2) additional three (3) years term. The only exceptions are the Chancellor of The Mico who shall serve for the duration of being Chancellor. The resident Trustee of The Lady Mico Trust, who shall serve as ex-officio member for the duration of his/her being Trustee, The President of The Mico who shall serve as ex-officio member for the duration of his/her tenure as President.

Professor The Honorable Errol Lawrence Miller OJ, CD.​

Trustee, The Lady Mico Trust.

Former Principal and Chancellor of The Mico College. Educator extraordinaire, visionary, author, and chief architect of The Mico Foundation. Professor Miller has provided leadership for the implementation of major reforms in the public education system. The Mico CARE Centre was his brainchild; he was also pivotal in initiating the female student’s hostel at Arnold Road, which now bears his name.

Dr. Sylvester Tulloch, CD

Chairman Board of Directors

Dr. Sylvester Tulloch is a proud graduate of The Mico. After a successful career in teaching, and then in the banking sector, Dr. Tulloch made the courageous decision to move into the Real Estate Development. Today he owns and manages Kemteek Development and Construction Limited, one of the most respected Real Estate Development Companies in Jamaica. Dr. Tulloch remains a committed Miconian and philanthropic individual offering support for the development of humanity. His annual scholarships for students of Mathematics at his Alma mater is but one of his several contributions to educational development. He is a committed Baptist and lay preacher who makes no apology for his Christian service.

Dr. R. Karl James CD.

Chancellor The Mico University College. Reprehensive of The Lady Mico Trust

Pharmacist, Businessman and Management Consultant extraordinaire. Dr. R. Karl James served The Mico Board of Directors for over 40 years. As Chairman of the Board he was pivotal to many of the developments that brought The Mico to University Cllege Status. His very keen interest in Special Education led to the establishment of the satellite CARE Centre in St. Anns Bay, which now bears the name. “Roderick Karl James CARE Centre” Dr. James represents the Lady Mico Trust on the Board of the Foundation.

Dr. Asburn Pinnock, J.P.

President: The Mico University College.

Dr. Asburn Pinnock assumed the position as President of The Mico University College, September 2015. As President, he sits as a Director on the Board of The Mico Foundation. Dr. Pinnock is an educational leader who served for over 30 years at various levels of the education system. He served as Principal of Sam Sharpe Teachers College before assuming the position as President of The Mico University College. 

Peter-John Gordon

Member

Dr. Peter-John Gordon is a member of the Economics Faculty of the University of the West Indies (Mona). He previously served as the Director of Economic Planning and Research at the Planning Institute of Jamaica. He has served on a number of public sector boards and was previously the chairman of the Jamaican Fair Trading Commission and the Maritime Authority of Jamaica. He serves The Mico Foundation as a representative for the Lady Mico Trust

Dr. Pansy Hamilton

Member

Dr. Pansy Hamilton is a Lecturer at the University of the West Indies, Mona, Jamaica. Assistant Director of the Hugh Wynter Fertility Management Unit, Faculty of Medical Sciences, UWI. She serves on The Mico Foundation Board as representative for the University College Board of Directors.

Mr.Burchell Duahney J.P.

Secretary Manager

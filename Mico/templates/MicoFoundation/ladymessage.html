Message

Chairman of The Lady Mico Trust

Rev. Rachel Benson DL

I am delighted that the Mico Foundation has decided to launch a new website to showcase the work of the Foundation and what the Mico University College is able to offer students in Jamaica and in the Caribbean. The Lady Mico Trust was founded by Sir Thomas Fowell Buxton in 1834 on Christian principles to support education in Jamaica. The Trustees endeavour to perpetuate his vision, which is well maintained in the advancements at the University College over the centuries. The University College stands on the original site which was given to the Mico Foundation by the Lady Mico Trust in 2000. The Lady Mico Trust now has a close relationship with The Mico Foundation who are the custodians of all the properties in which the University College operates.

The Trustees are proud of their long association with the University College and are pleased to work with the Mico Foundation in the provision of continuing support for the maintenance of the buildings and new projects which benefit the students and the College as a whole.

Message

I am delighted to welcome you to The Mico Foundation website where you will have the opportunity of learning more about who we are and what we do. Our primary goal is fixed on the strategic improvement of the assets of The Mico, the physical development and expansion of the property as a comfortable teaching and learning environment, the preservation of the Lady Mico legacy, and the main driver for endowment and entrepreneurial enterprises. The Foundation was incorporated in 1981 as a limited liability company not for profit, with its membership and its Board comprising of representatives nominated by the Lady Mico Trust, the Board of the University College, and the Mico Old Students Association. In 1991 the Lady Mico Trust in Great Britain entrusted to the Foundation the Mico estate at 1A Marescaux Road; this along with all other acquired premises makes it the owner and custodian of all The Mico estates in Jamaica. The primary objective of the Foundation is to preserve the heritage of the Lady Mico Trust, develop, expand and manage all acquired tangible and intangible assets and to raise funds to assist with the improvement of The Mico University College. In this regard, the Foundation commits to be an active, dependable and viable source for endowment, economic enterprises, assets preservation, and an enabling environment for the development of social capital.

I trust that as you browse through the website, you will be inspired by what we do as a charitable organization, the efforts which are made to facilitate the growth and development of the Mico University, and by extension education in the wider Jamaica and Caribbean Communities. The Foundation is always opened to new and productive ideas and invites you to share with us your ideas that will advance the interest of our objectives and those of the Mico University College.

Dr. Sylvester Tulloch, CD.

About us

The Mico Foundation started in 1981 as a Registered Charitable Organization with office at 1A Marescaux Road Kingston Jamaica. It was established to manage all non-Government assets of The Mico University College. The Foundation is custodian of the properties of the Lady Mico Trust and as such owns and manages all the properties in which the University College operates.

The Mico Foundation is led by a nine member Board of Directors that ensures proper management in support of the mission of the Mico University College and its outreach programmes. A Secretary Manager leads the day-to-day operations in the development and implementation of various projects. We earn and manage financial resources to support initiatives such as, research, infrastructure development, scholarship and grants, Technology and students financial aid.

The Foundation has initiated the development of an endowment fund as a strategic assets source to sustain the longevity of The Mico as a premier Caribbean Education Instruction.

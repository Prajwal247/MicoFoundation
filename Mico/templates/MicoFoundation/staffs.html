Our Staff

A workforce of seven-team members led by the Secretary Manager manages the daily affairs of The Mico Foundation.  The staff is guided by the vision and mission of the Foundation and is committed to provide the necessary support to ensure the highest quality service and the success of the projects that are undertaken.  The entrenched values of accountability, transparency, integrity and partnership are defining measures by which the staff conducts the business of the Foundation. 

Mr. Burchell Duhaney

Secretary Manager

Mr. Burchell Duhaney currently serves as the Secretary Manager of The Mico Foundation. He came to this position having served on the Board of Directors of the Foundation. He has distinguished himself in Educational, Leadership, and Management. He holds a Diploma in teaching from the Mico College, Bachelor of Science Degree (BSc) in Fine Art, a Masters of Science Degree (MSc) in Art Education from the Central Connecticut State University, a Masters of Science (MSc) in Instructional Technology and Distance Education from the Fischler School of Education, Nova Southeastern.

A Gift of land to the Mico Foundation

The Mico Foundation is the beneficiary of forty-seven acres of land in Barland in St. Thomas. Dr. Claudia Williams, daughter of the late Mr. Edwin Marcus Williams a graduate of the early 1900s bequeathed this property to the Mico Foundation. The title of the property was delivered to Dr. Arthur Geddes Chairman of The Mico Foundation Special Projects Committee on March 6,
2020.
